About me

Hi! My name is Dan. I originally hail from the great state of Connecticut. I am currently engaged in earning my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science at Marist College. Eventually I hope to do some original research, get a Master's degree and make the world a better place.



When I am not sitting in front of my battlestation writing code, I can usually be found playing my Trumpet (or French Horn, or Tuba!), camping, hiking or listening to jazz/classical music (though I am known to listen widely). However, even when I am not in school or at work, I can usually be found tinkering with the latest technologies. Technology is my first passion!



It all started when I built my first computer in the 8th grade. After troubleshooting and replacing a faulty motherboard I finally got the all-too welcoming POST message from the BIOS. I hurriedly inserted my family's Windows XP disc and installed it. Unfortunately, I learned a harsh lesson in licensing as my computer was effectively neutered after 30 days. The license was already in use on my parent's machine. So, I decided to try out this linux thing that I had heard a little bit about on the Internet. It was all down hill from there, but you can learn more about what I do now from my interests page.



If you are interested in working on a project with me, please stop by my contact page and drop me a line.
